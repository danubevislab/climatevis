Visualization Approaches to Climate Change

This is a collection of visualizations of climate change (CC) with a focus on representations for public audiences. It is the result from a Survey Study , which has been initiated by DanubeVISlab, and which is intended to steadily grow. Please send new candidates to mail adress (florian.windhager@donau-uni.ac.at).
